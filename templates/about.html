About Stellar Skies

Stellar Skies is a simple planet creator tool created in 24 hours by Drew Gifford and Jared Galyan for the University of Cincinnati's MakeUC 2020 event. Here, you can use our custom planet editor to create a collection of planets in your own star system and share your creations with friends.


Drew Gifford

Drew Gifford is an 18-year-old first-year computer engineering student at the University of Cincinnati. He has experience working on e-commerce and static websites in a professional environment.
